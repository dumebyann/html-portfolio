Best Movies According to Aanu

My Top movies of all time

Peaky Blinders

This is my favourite English movie. It is about a man Tommy shelby who indulges in illegal businesses and schemes to get a better life for himself and his family.
He sacrificed a lot in the long run, his mental health, his family among all.

Twenty five Twenty one

This is a korean coming of age love story between Na Hee Do and Baek Yi Jin. The chemistry was on point,excellent potrayal of emotions and including their relations with their friends.
In all, this drama teaches about love,friendship and relationship. you get a lot of butterflies in your tummy while watching it

Game of Thrones

really nice movie about struggle for power among nine noble families. The nine families fight for control over the Lands of Westeros, while an ancient enemy returns after being dormant for millenia.

Lord of the Rings

A series of three epic fantasy adventure films. It is a saga of a group of reluctant heroes who set forth to save their world from consummate evil.

The Great Gatsby

It tells the tragic story of a self-made millionaire and his pursuit of Daisy Buchanan, a wealthy young woman whom he loved in his youth.

Reply 1988

This korean series follows the lives of five families living on the same street in a neighbourhood called ssangmundong in seoul. It is a nostalgic look back at the year 1988.
